Lufus is a fully open source program and is developed and maintained by volunteers who spend their time on it as a hobby. Anyone is welcome to contribute and support Lufus as long as they meet our standards. Currently we are in need of contributors for:

Translation
Suggesting and developing new features
Providing support to others using the program
For translation, please open a PR with the translation file. You may also contact Hog on discord.
It is highly recommended to go through the whole documentation before trying to contribute to the source code. It is also important to go through B. Guidelines and Standards.
You can join our discord server to further enquire about anything lufus related.
